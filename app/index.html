Sylwia Karolina

About

Sylvia Karolina Dluzniewska is a fashion design student based in Copenhagen, Denmark. Her adventure with fashion and design began almost three years ago. She completed education as Design technologist, currently lives in the world of sustainable fashion, as both her ongoing education and job focus around sustainability in the fashion industry.

Her work primarily focuses around practical, simple solutions with a subtel touch of Scandinavian minimalism and a pinch of humour.

Filling her days with studying, part-time job and learning Danish, she also finds time to follow her hobbies: photography, travelling and graphic design.
